My Story

I was born in Hong Kong and grew up in San Jose, spending most of my childhood tinkering with my parents' computers. After graduating from SJSU in 2015 I worked in San Francisco doing accounting for a lively social impact focused coworking space, Mission Hub. I helped Mission Hub revamp their member tracking and billing system by being a lead in the implementation of Nexudus. This large undertaking required careful project planning regarding which features to utilize, UI layout, connection testing with payment gateways and metric reporting. The excitement of being adaptive and finding solutions during the Nexudus implementation lit a passion for development and I decided to make the pivot from accounting to web developement. I have since learned Javascript & Python, utilizing the MEAN and Django frameworks to build web apps. In my spare time I enjoy biking and going to the library for coding meetups.
